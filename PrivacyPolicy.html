How We Use Your Data

Effective Date: May 20 2025 Thank you for using Jailbreak 2. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information. 1. Who We Are Jailbreak 2 is an independent game/application developed and maintained by 1tsdr1ppy (Noah). This privacy policy applies to the app and all services provided through it. 2. Information We Collect Jailbreak 2 does not collect personal data directly from you. The only time we access limited personal information is through: Google Sign-In: We use Google as a secure way to allow users to sign in. This provides your Google account’s basic profile info (such as name and email), which is used only for authentication purposes. 3. How We Use Your Information We use Google account sign-in only to verify your identity. This makes logging in fast, secure, and user-friendly. We do not share, sell, or store any of your personal data on our own servers. 4. Third-Party Services Google Sign-In: Authentication is handled through Google's OAuth 2.0 system. You can read more about Google's Privacy Policy here. 5. Data Security We rely on Google's secure authentication systems. No sensitive user data is stored by Jailbreak 2. Any temporary tokens are used solely to establish secure sessions and are not retained long-term. 6. Children's Privacy Jailbreak 2 is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. 7. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with the updated effective date. 8. Contact If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at: Email: tsdrppy@gmail.com Website: https://the1tsdr1ppy.github.io

Game Rules & Community Guidelines 1. Stay On Topic This game is about cops and robbers—stick to that theme. Keep discussions relevant to the game; off-topic or disruptive conversations are not allowed. 2. Respectful Communication Only No harassment, threats, discrimination, or toxicity. Treat others with respect. No spamming, trolling, or intentionally ruining the experience for others. 3. Strict Voice Chat Moderation No cursing in voice chat. Keep it clean. Doxxing or sharing personal information is a serious offense—this will lead to an immediate ban. 4. Reports & Moderation All reports are handled within 24 hours. Advanced AI reviews reports and analyzes behavior patterns. If reported, your last 5 minutes of gameplay footage will be uploaded to moderators. Voice chat is actively recorded and monitored—if you break the rules, you will be caught. 5. No Cheating – Zero Tolerance Cheating, exploiting, or using third-party software is an instant permanent ban. We know if you’re using a VPN. Don’t bother trying to hide. 6. Consequences for Breaking Rules Warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the offense. If you cheat, expect a PERMANENT BAN—no appeals. By playing this game, you agree to these rules. Violations will not be tolerated.
